About SignBridge

Our Mission

SignBridge is a comprehensive platform for learning and recognizing sign language using AI-powered technology.

Technology Stack

  • Backend: Python FastAPI with Machine Learning
  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Recognition: TensorFlow/PyTorch (configurable)

Acknowledgments

Developed in collaboration with Dr. Anthony Chong and The Malaysian Sign Language and Deaf Studies National Organisation (MyBIM).
